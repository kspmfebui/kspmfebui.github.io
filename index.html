Explore Our Past Events

Delve into the essence of our past events—strategic competitions, insightful outlook sessions, and immersive company visits. Witness vibrant discussions, expert analyses, and shared practical wisdom.

Investalk

Discover the Indonesian Capital Market at Investalk—an exclusive investment event with expert speakers. Gain insights, connect with professionals, and elevate your understanding of financial markets. Don't miss out on this unique opportunity!

Sekolah Pasar Modal

Embark on a journey into the world of capital markets at Sekolah Pasar Modal. This exclusive event serves as a school for enthusiasts, featuring expert instructors who will impart valuable knowledge about the intricate workings of the capital market.

Comparative Study

Join us as we visit other universities' capital market study groups, engage in a knowledge exchange, gain insights, connect with peers, and broaden your understanding of capital markets through this collaborative initiative.

Company Visit

Join us for a captivating Company Visit, explore leading capital market-related firms. Gain practical insights, broaden perspectives, and connect with professionals.

Investment Outlook

Explore the financial landscape in our Investment Outlook session, featuring key experts. Gain insights into the current state of the capital market, with discussions on trends, forecasts, and investment opportunities.

ISTC: Indonesia Stock Trading Competition

Immerse yourself in Indonesia Stock Trading Competition, where participants navigate the dynamic capital market, analyzing and reporting their trading strategies.
