Kelompok Studi Pasar Modal Fakultas Ekonomi dan Bisnis Universitas Indonesia

Established in 2020, KSPM FEB UI embarked on a transformative journey dedicated to shaping the future of capital market education. In a relatively short span, we have evolved into a dynamic force, leaving a significant impact on the landscape of financial education.

As we reflect on our journey, each event and accomplishment serves as a testament to our dedication to fostering a community of informed investors and traders. Our vision of creating a space where students hone their interests, talents, and competencies in the capital market sector is gradually becoming a reality.

Looking ahead, we remain steadfast in our mission to instill the values of KSPM FEB UI, open new horizons of investment insights, and promote greater awareness of investment needs among students. The journey continues, and we invite you to be part of the exciting chapters yet to unfold.

We aim for students to hone their interests, talents, and competencies in the capital market sector through premier activities offered that are easy to access by enthusiasts in order to produce quality investors or traders while simultaneously raising awareness regarding business and the Indonesian economy in general.

1. Instill the values of KSPM FEB UI among the management and members.
2. Open horizons of investment insights for the short and long term.
3. Promote greater awareness of investment needs, especially among young students, fostering interest in financial markets.

In KSPM FEB UI, we prioritize professionalism, connectivity, and being advantageous. We actively contribute to FEB UI's environment through responsible time management, teamwork, and fostering a supportive atmosphere.

Professionalism

We manage time efficiently, exhibit appropriate behavior, and take responsibility for all work, producing consistent quality.

Connectivity

We create a supportive, productive, and communicative work atmosphere, committed to removing obstacles that impact the effectiveness of our team.

Advantageous

We expand capital market knowledge through active learning, self-leadership, and teamwork, contributing to the FEB UI environment.
