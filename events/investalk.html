Empowering Insights: Investalk

Step into the dynamic realm of finance with Investalk, a distinguished investment talk show proudly hosted by KSPM FEB UI. Designed to be an enriching experience for enthusiasts and professionals alike, Investalk brings together renowned experts from the capital market field to share their wealth of knowledge and insights.

In our latest Investalk episode, we had the privilege of hosting Lo Keng Hong, often referred to as the Warren Buffett of Indonesia. Mr. Lo Keng Hong's stature as a seasoned investor and his strategic prowess in the financial domain contributed to an enlightening and thought-provoking conversation. His experiences, successes, and unique perspective provided our audience with a rare glimpse into the strategies employed by one of Indonesia's most distinguished financial minds.

Investalk serves as a platform where cutting-edge discussions and in-depth analyses intersect, offering our audience a deeper understanding of investment landscapes. Join us on this captivating journey as we continue to feature prominent figures, exploring diverse aspects of finance and providing you with the knowledge to navigate the intricacies of the financial world. Investalk - where insightful conversations lead to informed decisions in the realm of investments.
